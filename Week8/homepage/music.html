Nostalgia

I'm deeply fascinated by the world of vaporwave, a genre that evokes nostalgia and a surreal reinterpretation of the past. Albums like 'News at 11' by Cat System Corp. captivate me with their unique blend of smooth jazz and ambient sounds, creating a parallel universe where time stands still. Artists like Ginger Root, Lush and Himiko Kikuchi transport me to a time that feels both familiar and unexplored. Their music, sometimes a dreamy rendition of the 80s and sometimes a serene dive into jazz, represents a journey through sound that resonates with my soul. I'm always feeling like taking a walk while listening to this kind of music.
